About NeuroSynca

NeuroSynca is a platform aimed at providing accessible testing and support for individuals in underserved Black and Latino communities. We focus on early diagnosis and intervention for autism in both children and adults.

Autism Statistics

Did you know:

  • 1 in 59 children in the U.S. is diagnosed with autism, but rates are lower among Black and Latino children due to lack of testing access.
  • The average cost of autism diagnosis ranges from $1,500 to $5,000, with limited coverage in minority communities.
  • Untreated autism in adulthood can lead to a higher unemployment rate and mental health challenges.
